Our All-Inclusive Services

We provide a seamless travel experience so you can focus on the beauty of Ethiopia while we handle the rest. Professional Transportation: Whether navigating the paved streets of Addis Ababa or the rugged terrain of the Bale Mountains Natinal Park, we provide safe and comfortable transport. Fleet: 4x4 Land Cruisers for off-road adventures and comfortable minivans for city tours. Experienced Drivers: Our drivers are experts in Ethiopian terrain and prioritize your safety. Airport Transfers: We provide "meet and greet" services at Bole International Airport. Expert Tour Guides: Our guides aren't just experts in history; they are storytellers who bring Ethiopia’s heritage to life. Local Knowledge: Deep insights into the rock-hewn churches, ancient ruins, and tribal traditions. Multilingual: We provide guides who speak English (and other languages upon request). ... Personalized Pace: We adapt the tour to your interests and energy levels. Premium Hotel & Lodging: We partner with the best accommodations in the country to ensure you have a restful night. Curated Selection: From luxury hotels in the city to authentic eco-lodges in the countryside. Pre-Vetted: We only book hotels that meet our standards for cleanliness, service, and location. Authentic Culinary Experiences: Food is a huge part of Ethiopian culture, and we make sure you taste the very best. Traditional Meals: Enjoy authentic Injera, Shiro, and Tibs at the best local spots. Coffee Ceremonies: Experience the traditional Ethiopian coffee ritual. Dietary Needs: We accommodate vegetarians, vegans, and any specific food allergies.
